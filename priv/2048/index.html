High Scores

How to play: This is a cooperative version of a popular 2048 game. Use your arrow keys to vote for a move. After a first player votes, 2 seconds countdown will start and the game will use the move with the highest vote count. In case of a tie, the move will be chosen randomly from the highest voted ones. After a game is finished (either lost or won), it will automatically restart in 10 seconds.

Created by Piotr Nosek (Erlang Solutions). Based on a 2048 game by Gabriele Cirulli.
